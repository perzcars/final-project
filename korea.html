South Korea: From Paju to Jeju

Date Notable Activities
05/20/24 Welcome dinner, N Seoul Tower, karaoke night.
05/21/24 Gyeongbokgung Palace, Bukchon Hanok Village, Insadong Art Street.
05/22/24 DMZ tour in Paju, Dora Observatory, evening in Hongdae, Myeongdong, Nanta show.
05/23/24 Train to Gyeongju, exploring Chunmachong and Cheomseongdae, Anapji Pond, stay the night in a traditional Korean hanok.
05/24/24 Golgulsa Temple, meditation sessions, archery and martial arts lessons, Buddhist vegetarian dinner.
05/25/24 Bus to Busan, Haedong Yonggung Temple, Gamcheon Culture Village, Songdo Marine Cable Car, Haeundae Beach.
05/26/24 Traditional cooking class in Busan, exploring the city, boat party with snacks and drinks.
05/27/24 Flight from Busan to Jeju Island, exploring the island.
05/28/24 Hiking to Seongsan Ilchubong (Sunrise Peak), Jeju Folk Village, Sangumburi crater, Jjeongbang Waterfall.
